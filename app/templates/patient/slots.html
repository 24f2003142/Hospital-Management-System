Provide Slot Availability

This page provides doctors to easily update the slots in which they are available so that the patient can book appointment.

  • The RED button specifies that the slot is booked by 3 or more patients.
  • The DISABLED button specifies that the slot is fully booked by patients.
  • The GREEN button specifies that the slot is available and can be selected to do the opening.
  • The BLUE button specifies that the slot is open for booking.
